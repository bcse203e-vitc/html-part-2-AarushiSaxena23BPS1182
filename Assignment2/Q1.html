Seasonal Treats

Author : Emma Frost | Date : 22nd December 2024

The holiday season is synonymous with indulgent, comforting sweets that bring warmth and joy to every celebration. Here are a few festive recipes to sweeten up your Christmas and New Year:

1. Gingerbread Cookies



Ingredients :
  1. 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tsp baking soda
  3. 1 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  4. 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  6. 1/2 tsp salt
  7. 3/4 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  8. 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  9. 1 large egg
  10. 1/2 cup molasses
  11. 1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions :
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and brown sugar until smooth. Add egg, molasses, and vanilla, and mix.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until combined.
  5. Roll out dough on a floured surface to about 1/4-inch thickness, and cut into shapes.
  6. Place cookies on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.

2. Fruitcake



Ingredients :
  1. 1 1/2 cups mixed dried fruit (raisins, currants, cranberries)
  2. 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)
  3. 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  4. 1 cup brown sugar
  5. 3 large eggs
  6. 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  7. 1 tsp baking powder
  8. 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  9. 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  10. 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  11. 1/4 cup orange juice
  12. 1/4 cup dark rum
Instructions :
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease and line a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine dried fruit and nuts.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  4. Mix in flour, baking powder, spices, orange juice, and rum.
  5. Fold in the dried fruit and nuts.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool before serving.

3. Sparkling Sugar Donuts



Ingredients :
  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  3. 1 tsp baking powder
  4. 1/4 tsp salt
  5. 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  7. 1/2 cup whole milk
  8. 1/4 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  9. 1 large egg
  10. 1/2 cup sparkling sugar (for rolling)
Instructions :
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a donut pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. In another bowl, combine milk, melted butter, and egg.
  4. Gradually add wet ingredients to dry ingredients, mixing until smooth.
  5. Spoon the batter into the donut pan and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Let donuts cool slightly, then roll in sparkling sugar for a festive touch.

Key Takeaways :

  • Gingerbread cookies and fruitcake are classic Christmas treats with rich, spiced flavors that evoke holiday cheer.
  • Champagne cupcakes and sparkling sugar donuts add a celebratory touch for New Year, with light, festive flavors.
  • Baking these treats is a great way to share love and joy during the holiday season, whether for family, friends, or yourself!
