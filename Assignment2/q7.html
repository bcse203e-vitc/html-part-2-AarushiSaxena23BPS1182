Community Gathering 2024

Schedule | Activities | Register

Event Details

Join us for the annual Community Gathering! This event aims to bring people together, celebrate local culture, and enjoy fun activities for all ages. Whether you're looking to participate in exciting workshops or simply enjoy some good food and company, there's something for everyone.

Location & Date

Location: City Park, Downtown

Date: June 5, 2024

Main Activities

  • Community Picnic: Bring your blankets and enjoy a relaxing afternoon in the park with fellow attendees.
  • Workshops: Participate in a variety of skill-building workshops such as painting, cooking, and gardening.
  • Live Performances: Local musicians and performers will entertain you throughout the day.
  • Food Trucks: A variety of food trucks will be on-site offering delicious snacks and meals for purchase.
  • Family Games: Fun games and activities for kids and adults alike, including tug-of-war and relay races.

Featured Activities

During the event, you can enjoy a variety of fun and engaging activities:

  • Cooking Class: Learn how to make delicious dishes using local, fresh ingredients.
  • Arts & Crafts Booths: Create your own unique crafts with supplies provided by local artists.
  • Yoga Session: A relaxing group yoga class open to all skill levels.
  • Petting Zoo: A family-friendly petting zoo with adorable animals.
  • Sports Tournament: Compete in friendly matches of soccer and volleyball.
